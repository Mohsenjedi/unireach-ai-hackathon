# Marketing Sample Ads Directory

This directory should contain language-specific sample ad images for each country market. The marketing strategy page automatically detects the language for each country and displays the corresponding ad sample.

## Image Files Required

Add your sample ad images with the following filenames:

- `cn-ad.jpg` - Chinese market ad (detected: 中文)
- `in-ad.jpg` - India market ad (detected: English)
- `kr-ad.jpg` - Korean market ad (detected: 한국어)
- `vn-ad.jpg` - Vietnamese market ad (detected: Tiếng Việt)
- `eg-ad.jpg` - Egyptian market ad (detected: العربية)
- `br-ad.jpg` - Brazilian market ad (detected: Português)
- `pk-ad.jpg` - Pakistani market ad (detected: اردو)
- `ng-ad.jpg` - Nigerian market ad (detected: English)
- `ml-ad.jpg` - Mali market ad (detected: Français)
- `mm-ad.jpg` - Myanmar market ad (detected: မြန်မာ)

## How It Works

1. When a user clicks on a country in the marketing strategy page
2. The detected language for that country is displayed
3. The corresponding sample ad image appears below the language label
4. The language detection is automatic based on country code mapping

## Image Specifications

- Format: JPG/PNG (preferably JPEG for better performance)
- Recommended size: 400x200px or wider aspect ratio
- Ensure text/content is clear and readable at smaller sizes
- Consider localized messaging in each ad

## Language Mapping

The app automatically maps each country to its primary language:
- China → Chinese (中文)
- India → English
- South Korea → Korean (한국어)
- Vietnam → Vietnamese (Tiếng Việt)
- Egypt → Arabic (العربية)
- Brazil → Portuguese (Português)
- Pakistan → Urdu (اردو)
- Nigeria → English
- Mali → French (Français)
- Myanmar → Burmese (မြန်မာ)
